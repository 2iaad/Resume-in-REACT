# Resume pattern

1. Full name
2. Contacts
3. Summary
4. Skills:
	- Languages:
	- Frameworks:
	- Tools:
5. Work experience:
	- Job title - company name.
	- What i did, how i did it, impact it made. 
	- Technologies used.
6. Education:
	- Institue - Period.
	- Title.
	- Description.
7. Projects:
	- Webserv.
	- Minishell.

8. Awards or certifications
